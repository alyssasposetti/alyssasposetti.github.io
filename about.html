Who is Alyssa Sposetti?

Alyssa Sposetti is a first year student at The Cleveland Institute of Art. Her intended major is illustration, but she also works with various different styles and mediums. When she isn't making art or coding websites at one in the morning, she enjoys doing stuff with her friends and constantly creating cool stuff for people to enjoy

Sposetti is from Youngstown, Ohio, and grew up with a paint brush in her hand. Her art education started from early childhood and continued into high school where she excelled in digital programs, as well as dabbling in digital art.
